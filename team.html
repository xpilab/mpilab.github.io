SM

Shweta Mahajan

Principal Investigator · Assistant Professor, EECS · Vector Faculty Affiliate

Prof. Mahajan's research spans deep generative models for multimodal learning — diffusion models, normalizing flows, and VAEs — with applications to vision, language, and autonomous systems. She completed her Ph.D. (summa cum laude) at TU Darmstadt and was a postdoctoral researcher at UBC and a researcher at Qualcomm AI Research.
