Teaching Philosophy

My goal is to give students both a rigorous mathematical foundation and hands-on experience with the latest tools in computer vision and machine learning. Courses are structured around a mix of lectures, paper discussions, and programming projects that mirror real research workflows.

I believe the best learning happens when students engage critically with ideas — questioning assumptions, reproducing results, and proposing extensions. I encourage all students, regardless of background, to ask questions and participate actively.
