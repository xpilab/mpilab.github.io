Join MPI Lab

We are a young and growing lab at York University working on computer vision, generative models, and multimodal learning. We welcome motivated students and researchers who are passionate about pushing the boundaries of visual AI.

  • Undergrads: send a brief email with your transcript and a description of your interests

How to Apply

Before emailing, please read our recent publications to get a sense of the lab's research directions. In your email, include:


  • Your CV / resume
  • A brief paragraph describing your research interests and why you want to join MPI Lab
  • Links to any previous research output (papers, code, thesis)
  • Unofficial transcripts (for graduate applicants)
Note on response time: Due to a high volume of enquiries, Prof. Mahajan may not be able to reply to every email individually. Applicants who closely match the lab's research interests will be contacted for a follow-up conversation.

Life at MPI Lab & York

MPI Lab is part of the Lassonde School of Engineering at York University in Toronto, Canada — one of the world's most diverse and multicultural cities. We are affiliated with the Vector Institute for Artificial Intelligence, providing access to a broad network of AI researchers across academia and industry.

We foster an inclusive, collaborative, and intellectually stimulating environment. Lab members are encouraged to attend top-tier conferences, collaborate with industry partners, and engage with the wider Vector Institute community.
