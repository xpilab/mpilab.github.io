Frequently Asked Questions

Are positions funded?

PhD positions are fully funded through a combination of TA/RA salary and research grants. Masters funding varies — please inquire directly.

What research topics does the lab focus on?

Our primary focus is on deep generative models (diffusion models, normalizing flows, VAEs) for multimodal representation learning, with applications to image synthesis, video generation, correspondence, and autonomous systems.

I'm an international student — can I apply?

Absolutely. York University welcomes international students and has strong support infrastructure. Please review the international admissions requirements on the York graduate portal.

Do I need a publication to apply?

Publications are a strong asset but not required for PhD applications. Strong undergraduate research projects, thesis work, or open-source contributions are also valued.
